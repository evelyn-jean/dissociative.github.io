Treatment Options

  • Psychotherapy - Also known as talk therapy, individuals diagnosed with dissociative disorders can seek this form of therapy to help with their condition. Psychotherapy is the main form of treatment for dissociative disorder and involves the individual talking with a professional about their disorder and related issues. The therapists work with the individuals to help them understand the causes of their disorders and ways to cope with stress and stressful situations. In time, the therapists may be able to help the individuals work through the traumatic events they went through.
  • Medicine - there are no specific medications that treat dissociative disorders, although doctors may prescribe other medications such as antidepressants, anti-anxiety medicines or antipsychotic medicines, which may help with some of the symptoms of dissociative disorders.
