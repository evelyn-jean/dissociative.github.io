Organisations dedicated to dissociative disorders

Multiplied By One Org

Multiplied By One Org is an organisation based in Canada that provides information and different services related to dissociative disorders. Multiplied By One Org provides many types of services like support groups, a directory for therapists, information, and other resources. Multiplied By One Org was created for Canadians who have a dissociative disorder or know someone with a dissociative disorder, as Canada doesn’t have that many organisations or institutions with a lot of reliable information about these disorders.

an infinite mind

an infinite mind is an organisation dedicated to educating people on dissociative disorders and providing support and resources to people with a dissociative disorder. an infinite mind provides informative events and conferences for people with dissociative disorders and their loved ones, as well as providing stories from people who have or have had a dissociative disorder to further educate people on what dissociative disorders are and what it is like living with one.
